Why I started this blog

  1. Photography

    Photography has been a hobby of mine for almost 10 years now, starting when I was just 11.

    Specifically capturing cameras with film. The first film camera that I've ever had was the Pentax K1000.

    It was one of the most beautifully designed cameras that I've ever owned. Following that was the Canon canonet, and then the Canon AE-1.

    I want to show the world what I see thru my eyes, and a camera does a very good job at doing that.

  2. To improve my terrible writing skills

    I've at least created 3 blogs throughout my lifetime for the sole purpose of bettering my writing.

    And none of them have lasted at all. I hope this time will be the last blog that I create.

    Not only that, but I have been inspired by a group of awesome people to create a blog.

    I've read their blog throughout the entirety of it, and I have essentially seen just how much they have beautifully progressed throughout their life.

    When I'm old, I hope to read my own story and progress.

  3. To show incredible passion for technology

    I am extremely passionate about new technologies.

    I am a big fan of

    • Cars
    • New programming philosophy/techniques
    • Hydroponics

    I want to show my passion of these kinds of technology in this blog.

    Currently, I am implementing a vacuumless brake booster, and electric power steering to create a self driving car using Comma.AI

    I hope to create a post on it soon.

Although my reasons are few, they contain passion. I hope that I can translate my passion in this blog.

Have a great day.
